about our restaurant

The All American Bar and Grill is one of the finest eating and drinking establishments on Cape Cod. Try our seafood, steak, ribs, nightly specials and many other menu choices. Our friendly, professional staff will make sure that your dining experience with us will be exceptional.

Catering and Takeout Available - give us a call at (508) 394-1119. We accept all major credit cards.

Enjoy the beautiful Cape Cod outdoors while dining on our deck. We serve our full food and drink menu outside so you can take advantage of the beautiful weather. We also have KENO and a television on the deck.
