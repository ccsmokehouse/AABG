The All American Bar and Grill on Cape Cod has earned a strong reputation as a friendly, comfortable place to hang out and enjoy great food and drink at an excellent price. Our friendly, professional staff works hard to make sure that you enjoy an exceptional dining experience every time you walk through our doors.

Additionally, we feature outdoor dining, KENO and have five televisions so that you can watch your favorite sports teams. Catering and Takeout is available, just give us a call at (508) 394-1119.

The All American Bar and Grill is located on Cape Cod in South Dennis, MA.
